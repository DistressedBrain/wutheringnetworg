What is Wuthering Showcase?

Wuthering Showcase is a website created for players to show their character builds summerized into one single image. The website works like this: You go to "UID" put your game id and press the arrow to contimue. When you did these steps, you are on a page with all of your in-game details showing all of your characters that you own. You just have to select wich character you want a summerize and THATS IT!! PS. you can also choose a pic for your character portfolio from your saved pics :3
